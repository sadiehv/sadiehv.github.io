Kelly Cundiff began hand-knitting wire on tiny knitting needles in 2010 when her family decided the weather in Seattle was too warm to wear sweaters. After much experimenting she settled into a method which involves pre-stringing beads onto fine wire in reverse order of the predetermined pattern. Each piece requires a minimum investment of 10 hours and some have taken up to a week to complete. Her materials have evolved to include fine silver and semiprecious stones. These cuff bracelets and drop necklaces offer unique and elegant fashion statements that garner attention whenever they are worn.
